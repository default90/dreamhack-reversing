## Quiz_1
**Question**:   
What is the value of AX if RAX is 0x0123456789abcdef?   
**solution**:   
RAX: 64-bit register, 0x0123456789abcdef   
EAX: Low 32-bit register, 0x89abcdef   
AX: Low 16-bit register, 0xcdef   
**Answer**:   
0xcdef
